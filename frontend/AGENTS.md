# Project Overview

This repository is for **Paikan / Phuket Local Quest Platform**, an innovation MVP for sustainable tourism in Phuket.

The product should not be treated as a generic travel directory, map clone, TripAdvisor clone, or normal tourism landing page. The core idea is a **web-first local quest platform** that turns Phuket local communities, OTOP products, local restaurants, wellness services, hidden places, and community experiences into short gamified travel routes.

Primary mission:

- Help distribute tourism attention and income beyond the most famous Phuket hotspots.
- Support local partners by bringing tourists to local businesses and community experiences.
- Measure real impact through check-ins, quest completions, coupon redemptions, partner visits, and estimated local spending.

Current MVP direction:

- Start with a responsive Web App / PWA, not a native mobile app.
- Phuket is the MVP area.
- English-first UI for tourists.
- Focus on People and Profit from the Southern Region tourism sustainability theme.
- Use a small MVP scope: a few quest routes, local place cards, QR/check-in flow, rewards/coupons, partner dashboard, admin/impact dashboard.
- Prioritize a usable MVP over a large feature set.

Important product positioning:

> A Phuket local quest platform that turns hidden OTOP products, community services, wellness activities, and local restaurants into gamified travel routes with measurable income distribution.

Thai positioning:

> แพลตฟอร์ม Local Quest ที่เปลี่ยนสินค้า OTOP บริการชุมชน wellness และร้านท้องถิ่นในภูเก็ต ให้กลายเป็นเส้นทางท่องเที่ยวแบบภารกิจ พร้อมระบบสะสมแต้ม แลกรางวัล และวัดผลการกระจายรายได้สู่พื้นที่จริง

Build principles:

- Do not overbuild.
- Do not add heavy AI recommendation, payment, native app, or full map discovery until the MVP is validated.
- Do not make the product only a directory of places.
- Keep tourist flows short and low-friction.
- Prefer QR/code check-in and simple coupon redemption for the first version.
- Make impact measurement visible and understandable for competitions, partners, and stakeholders.
- Use realistic mock data when real partner data is not available yet.
